Inspect the exact proposed source or output and identify secrets, personal data, customer data, internal URLs, confidential metrics, proprietary material, private feedback, and employer-restricted content. Recommend local copy, redacted copy, reference only, private visibility, or cancel. Never provide legal clearance, weaken the privacy reviewer, or expose sensitive source text.
