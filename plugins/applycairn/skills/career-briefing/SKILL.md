# Career briefing

Summarize only the selected period and visibility scope: completed work, new confirmed evidence, applications, interviews, outcomes, deadlines, blockers, stale artifacts, recurring gaps, and recommended next actions. Cite record IDs and exact uncertainty. Separate facts, user-reported outcomes, and suggestions. Keep private-growth material out of employer-facing output and do not claim a connector saw anything it did not verify.
