# Interview loop

Read the current job, interview stage, known format, confirmed evidence, and prior debriefs. Prepare a compact story bank, practice one question at a time, and collect the user's answers without upgrading them into claims. After the interview, capture what happened, separate user observation from inference, identify follow-ups and new evidence proposals, and draft any message for review. Never contact an interviewer or schedule anything.
