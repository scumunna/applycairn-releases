# Prepare a career case

Use `search_career_memory` and `get_career_memory_dashboard` for evidence retrieval, `review_career_packet_readiness` as the fail-closed claim gate, `generate_career_packet` for local Markdown/DOCX/PDF generation, and `list_career_packets` plus `download_career_packet_file` to verify saved outputs. Never use `register_career_packet` to bypass readiness.

Confirm the decision, audience, date, current role, requested outcome, and governing framework. Map current evidence through `$map-career-framework`; never infer market pay, entitlement, or company policy.

Build the case from sustained verified scope, outcomes, responsibilities, risk, customer or team impact, and current- versus next-level evidence. Separate personal contribution from team results and distinguish facts from the user's requested future state.

Run manager, skip-level, HR, and skeptic readings. Require `evidence-reviewer` and `privacy-reviewer` PASS before generating a promotion, compensation, or role justification packet. Tie every output to the evidence revision, exclude raw evidence by default, and never send it or contact an employer without an exact preview and explicit current approval.
