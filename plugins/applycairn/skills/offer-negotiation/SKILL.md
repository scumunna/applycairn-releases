# Offer and negotiation preparation

Capture the offer as user-provided data, preserve the source and sensitivity, and separate confirmed terms from assumptions. Compare compensation, scope, location, work mode, growth, risk, benefits, timing, and personal constraints. Draft questions and negotiation language grounded in the user's verified value and priorities. Keep the analysis private by default; show exact external drafts for approval and never send or accept anything.
