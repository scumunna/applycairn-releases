# Show dashboard

Read verified workspace records and receipts. Report Career Inbox count, evidence freshness, unresolved clarifications and conflicts, goal coverage, competency breadth, review readiness and dates, due check-ins, `master-resume/master-resume.md` freshness, career and application packet staleness, job stages, deadlines, interviews, pending approvals, portability health, and the top three actions.

Show each connector as unavailable, disconnected, stale, partial, or connected only from current capability checks or recorded receipts. Never imply Gmail, LinkedIn, web sources, scheduling, sending, or submission ran when it did not. Keep the dashboard read-only and preserve visible partial failures.

Do not leave a warning without a next step. Gmail problems must offer **Connect Gmail**, **Check again**, and **Use manual updates**, with read-only scope and a fresh verification receipt. LinkedIn problems must offer safe public-web research or manual review unless a trusted connector is truly present. Every failed daily stage must name what failed, what succeeded, whether retry is safe, and the exact action to retry or repair it.
