This matrix is the source of truth for the website, GitHub README, listings,
release notes, and LinkedIn launch copy for v0.4.0. “Verified” means supported by
the current source, packaged runtime, or automated test evidence. It does not erase
the limitation in the last column.

## Claims that must not ship

- “Fully autonomous” without the unattended-action and host-availability limits.
- “Connects to LinkedIn,” “scrapes recruiters,” or “automatically messages people.”
- “Automatically applies” or “sends email automatically.”
- “Continuously monitors your work,” “connects every workplace tool,” or
  “automatically builds a verified career record.”
- “Guarantees your promotion,” “proves your level,” or “gets you a raise.”
- “Guaranteed to find every job,” “guaranteed ATS pass,” or “guaranteed interview.”
- “Free” without stating that ApplyCairn charges no fee and host costs may apply.
- “No data leaves your computer.”
- “Secure,” “encrypted,” “signed,” “externally audited,” “WCAG compliant,” or
  “mass-use ready” until the corresponding release gates are actually closed.
- Testimonials, customer counts, usage metrics, employer logos, partner logos, or
  production-success claims without real permission and evidence.
